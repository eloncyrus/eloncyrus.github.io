HOW TO BUY

1. Download the Trust Wallet (App) or MetaMask (Browser PlugIn and App) on your smartphone or browser, install the wallet and make a secure backup of your key phrase.

2. Log in to Binance (or create a new account).

3. Make sure you own BNB (buy it if needed) and go to the BNB Coin withdraw page.

4. If you are using TrustWallet you skip this step. In MetaMask go to the Settings Page and select the Tap “Networks“. Click “Add Network” and enter the following data:

Network Name: Smart Chain

New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/

ChainID: 56

Symbol: BNB

Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

5. Open your Trust Wallet or MetaMask, click on Smart Chain and then on Receive. Copy the wallet address.

6. Go to Binance again, paste the address at the Recipient’s BNB Address, select Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and send your BNB to your Trust Wallet or MetaMask.

7. Give Binance some time to send your coins.

8. After you received the coins, check Trust Wallet if Browser is visible between Wallet and DEX. If not, go to Safari and type trust://browser_enable to activate the dApp Browser. Click on Browser.

9. Click on Pancakeswap.

10. Click on Connect (sometimes you are automatically connected and sometimes you are not).

11. Click on V2 and type in 12% for slippage.

12. At the top, enter the number of BNB you want to convert to ELONCYRUS.

13. Below that field, click on Select a currency, paste ADDRESS and ELONCYRUS will appear.

14. Click on Swap.
